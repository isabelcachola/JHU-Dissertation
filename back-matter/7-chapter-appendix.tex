\section{Human Evaluation Compensation}\label{sec:ch-7-annotator-comp}
As the recruited annotators for the human evaluation presented in~\Cref{chap:chap-7} were volunteers, they were not compensated monetarily. However, annotators local to Baltimore were compensated confectionerily. One annotator commented ``the annotations are easy and useful and the treats are YUMMY.''
